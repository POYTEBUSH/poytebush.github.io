About Me:

Hi, my name is Peter and I am currently studying in the final year of BSc. Computer Games Programming at Staffordshire University, learning different aspects of games development whilst being able to specialise in my interest of AI. I have always wanted to work in the Games Industry.

Growing up playing GoldenEye, Spyro and Crash Bandicoot on the PS1 captivated me without understanding how games are made that it was what I wanted to do. I have really enjoyed my studies and would like to continue my lifelong ambition into the industry. Most of my work is available via my GitHub, some repositories may be private due to them being part of a University module. If you would like to view any private repository or have any questions, feel free to email me.
